Privacy policy

All the Android apps from https://play.google.com/store/apps/dev?id=6596947391073936664 are developed and maintained by Romain Pellerin, referred to as the developer.

The above-mentioned apps are, namely:

  • Learn A Foreign Language
  • SMS Reader
  • Remote control via SMS

The developer currently resides in San Francisco, California, USA.

He can be contacted over email. You can find his email address at the bottom of this webpage: http://romainpellerin.eu.

User data

Among the aforementioned apps, only Learn A Foreign Language collects user data, that is to say:

  • Email address
  • Password
These elements must be created by users who want to use the app. They do not allow the developer to identify users whatsoever. This information is stored on a server hosted by https://www.openshift.com/.

Deletion of this elements is possible by a mere request over email.
